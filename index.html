About DevCon Mandalay

Developer Conference or DevCon Mandalay is one of the well known events for everyone who interested in programming and software development technologies, students and youths who are studying to start their career as a Developer or Software Engineer or a Programmer. During the conference, most well-known professionals in IT industry of Myanmar will be sharing their experiences and uprising software development technologies for different application platforms (Window, Web, Mobile). Moreover, we are gonna disucss about current technology trends and best practices as a Professional Developer.

Objectives?

  • Get to know what are trending and powerful software development tools from the best developers in Myanmar
  • Build your ideas with right technology platform
  • Explore your technical knowledge
  • Become better developer life with effective codeing skills
